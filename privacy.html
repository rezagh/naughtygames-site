Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Key Privacy Points

  • ✓ No account or login required to use the app
  • ✓ All game data is stored locally on your device
  • ✓ We do not collect personal information
  • ✓ We never sell your data

1. Introduction

Naughty Games ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally

  • Player Names: Names you enter are stored only on your device and never sent to our servers
  • Game Progress: Which prompts you've seen, ratings, and session data are stored locally on your device
  • Purchase State: Records of unlocked levels and subscription status are stored on your device

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Game Content Sync: The app fetches game content (prompts, dice faces) from our server. We log standard server access information (IP address, timestamp) but do not associate this with any personal identity

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT require account creation or login
  • We do NOT collect names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do NOT read or transmit your game activity to our servers

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely through Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information. All billing is handled by the respective platform.

4. Data Storage

All personal data (player names, game progress, preferences) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device. If you uninstall the app, this data is permanently deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal information to third parties. The only network communication is fetching game content from our server, which does not include any personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for adults only (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used this app, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. All your data is on your device and under your control. You can clear it by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@naughtygames.app
